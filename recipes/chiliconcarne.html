Chilli Recipe by R00trick

Description

This Chilli is my own special recipe handed down generations from my grandfather's grandfather's grandfather.

It's easy and simple! And you'll have delicious leftovers for days!

Ingredients

Here's what you'll need:

  • Ground beef
  • Chilli powder
  • Bacon
  • 1 Onion
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • Olive oil
  • Red/White kidney beans
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Worchestershire sauce
  • Vegetables: celery, carrot, peas, corn
  • Spices: cumin, cayenne, salt, pepper

Cooking Instructions

  1. Begin by cooking your bacon until nice crisp. Set aside
  2. Then dice up onion and garlic cloves, set aside for now
  3. Next, brown your ground beef using oily fat from bacon. mmh, mmh delicious!
  4. Once browned, remove from pan. Drain excess water if neccessary
  5. Add a bit of olive oil, then begin cookin onions and garlic until savory aroma enters the room
  6. Return ground beef, stir, and then crumble bacon and add it as well
  7. After a few minutes, add worchester sauce, can of tomatoes (water included), and tomato paste. Stir until mixed
  8. Move mixture to larger pot. Add kindey beans, chilli powder, veggies, and rest of your spices.
  9. Add water, bring to boil and let cook for 15 mins.
  10. Reduce heat, let simmer and stew for at least one hour. The longer the better!
  11. Serve with shredded cheddar cheese and sour cream!
