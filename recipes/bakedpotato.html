Baked Potato

This is my famous baked potato! So good, you'll slap you're grandma!

Ingridients/Supplies

  • 1 potato
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Shreeded Cheese
  • Butter
  • Chives

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F. Use a fork to poke holes on two sides of the potato
  2. Wrap potato in aluminum foil
  3. Place wrapped potato into oven for 30 mins or until soft and squishy
  4. Remove potato from oven. Unwrap and use fork or knife to split it open
  5. Add butter, salt, pepper, cheese, chives, and sour cream
  6. Boom! you're potato is ready

If you really want to take things up a notch, you can cook bacon, chop it up, and throw it on top!
